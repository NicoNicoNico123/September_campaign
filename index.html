Whac-A-Mole Campaign

A Gamified Strategy to Drive Sales & Email Subscriptions

This proposal outlines a multi-layered digital campaign for the Hong Kong adult wellness market, leveraging gamification, influencer marketing, and targeted ads to boost engagement and conversion.

The Campaign Mechanism

Our strategy combines three core pillars: a fun gamified activation to attract users, a multi-tiered offer system to incentivize purchases, and a powerful KOL-driven promotion to build trust and reach.

Multi-Tiered Offer Strategy

To maximize conversion, we will deploy a variable offer strategy. This allows us to create urgency with limited high-value discounts via KOLs, reward engagement from game players, and capture a broad audience with a low-barrier-to-entry offer.
